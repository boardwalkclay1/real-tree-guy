Real work. Real people. Real Tree Guy.

Real Tree Guy is an app and a movement. I’m Chainsaw Clay, and I move through the city helping people clean up their yards, remove dangerous limbs, and bring life back to spaces that got forgotten.

This site is where we tell those stories, raise money for people who need help, and show you exactly where your support goes.

The Real Tree Guy App

The app lets you:

  • Book cleanups: Request yard work, tree trimming, and debris removal.
  • Follow stories: See real clients in the city and the work we do together.
  • Support others: Donate directly to jobs for people who can’t afford the full cost.

About Chainsaw Clay

I’m Chainsaw Clay—real tree guy, real work, real heart. I started this because I kept seeing people who needed help: overgrown yards, dangerous branches, and no money to fix it.

Instead of just scrolling past, I picked up my saw and started showing up. The app and this website exist so we can turn that energy into a system: you see the story, you see the work, you see the result.

Why this website exists

This isn’t just a promo page. This is our hub:

  • Transparency: Every project shows the client, the yard, the cost, and the progress.
  • Community: You can donate directly to specific jobs and see them get completed.
  • Recognition: Everyone who donates gets listed and tagged with a special badge.

When we hit a goal, we do the job, post the photos, and mark it complete. No mystery—just real work and real impact.
